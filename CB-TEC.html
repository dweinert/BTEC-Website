About Bollman Technical Education Center

"Bollman Tech's career and technical educational programs prepare students for today’s competitive and dynamic workplace by providing them with essential post-secondary and workforce readiness skills. Each program has an active advisory committee comprised of business professionals, community members, educators and students. These committees provide a current perspective on industry standards and expectations."

Career and Technical Education

"Career and Technical Education educators in Adams 12 Five Star Schools are working on a district curriculum alignment project. The purpose of our work is to answer four fundamental questions (based on Professional Learning Communities):

1) What do we want our students to know?
2) How will we know when they have learned what we want them to?
3) What do we do when students haven't learned what we want them to know?
4) What do we do when students have learned what we want them to know?"
